Goals for Starship Robotics

💻 Hardware

In Starship Robotics, our goal is to invent life-changing robotics. For hardware, we use Arduino to create robotics, each invention includes a prototype that you can find. Each prototype has taken months to bring it to life. Currently, we are taking the first step in gaining attention in Malaysia, Sandakan. We are always seeking funding, which is open to the public to donate, and every donation is greatly appreciated for helping a student reach his goals.

📱 Software

In Starship Robotics, software programming is made to bring apps alive. For software, Starship Robotics will make websites, games, or apps. We are open for commissions to make your website, games, or apps through the commission tab. Currently, we are taking the first step in gaining attention in Malaysia, Sandakan. We are always seeking funding, which is open to the public to donate, and every donation is greatly appreciated for helping a student reach his goals.
